The mix of indoor/outdoor venue coincides with the clash of Capulet/Montague feud, and provides an immersive experience for the audience as the tale unfolds. Dancers and musicians weave in and out of the crowd as explosive stunts and fire performance turn heads. (Exit JULIET) brings you on a life-affirming journey in a wordless, heart racing extravaganza that will leave you spell-bound. A love story to defy the stars, (Exit JULIET) will move and astound you.

Emotion in Motion

A Hip-Hop / modern dance interpretation of Romeo and Juliet performed by Dance Republic in Dublin’s Meeting House Square accompanied by live Hip-Hop Orchestra String Bling. This is the story of Romeo and Juliet as you’ve never seen it before. Cutting-edge choreography coupled with passionate String Quartets, Drumbeats and Pyrotechnics, (Exit JULIET) is a street music and dance explosion. Meeting House Square is the ideal venue with dark alleyways and corners for dancers to perform and interact on and off the stage
